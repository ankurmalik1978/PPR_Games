Guide to the knockout challenge

Tournament Overview

Welcome to the official guide for our multi-sport tournament in PPR. This event features Tennis, Cricket, Football, Badminton, and TT. Each sport is structured as a high-stakes, single-elimination knockout tournament.

While individual sports are knockout-based, the overall tournament champion is determined by a cumulative points system. Teams earn points for wins in the various events. Please see Format for more details


There is a special individual walking event. This will be under 2 age groups: "1st: between 45 and 55 years" and "2nd : > 55". It will not be part of the team scoring, but a separate prize will be awarded to the individual who covers the longest distance during the duration of the tournament.


Use the navigation to explore the tournament format, team rules, the full schedule, scoring system, and to track live standings on the leaderboard.

Knockout Format Explained

Overall event is team based. All teams need to follow the criteria mentioned below to form their teams:

  • 1. All blocks (Block A, B, C, D and E) need to come up with a "Team", "Team Name" and a "Captain".
  • 2. All team members need to be from the same block.
  • 3. We want good participation from all. Hence, each team needs to have at least 10 members which should include at least 2 ladies and 2 kids < 16.
  • 4. All teams need to play all events. Hence, teams should be formed in a way so that all sports could be covered
