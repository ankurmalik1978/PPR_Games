Tournament Overview

Welcome to the official hub for our multi-sport tournament. This event features Tennis, Cricket, Football, Volleyball, Badminton, Snooker, and a walking challenge. Each sport is structured as a high-stakes, single-elimination knockout tournament.

While individual sports are knockout-based, the overall tournament champion is determined by a cumulative points system. Teams earn points for wins and participation across all events. Use the navigation to explore the tournament format, team rules, the full schedule, scoring system, and to track live standings on the leaderboard.

Knockout Format Explained

Each sport follows a single-elimination knockout format. This means a team is eliminated from that specific sport's bracket after a single loss. The winner of each match advances to the next round until one champion remains. Below is a simple example for a 4-team bracket.

Team Composition

To ensure balanced competition, all teams must adhere to the following composition rules. Each team is capped at 8 players, with specific requirements for age and gender to promote inclusivity.

  • Max Players: 8 per team
  • Mandatory: Minimum 2 ladies/girls
  • Mandatory: Minimum 2 boys (age 10-16)
  • Football: 5 players + 3 substitutes
  • Cricket: 6 players + 2 substitutes
  • Walking: Entire team can participate
